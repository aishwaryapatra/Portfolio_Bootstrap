Hello There!

Welcome to my page.

If you are moving with a restless heart then you are alive.If you carry the fervour of dreams in your eyes then you are alive.

Our dreams have been doctored.We belong nowhere.We sail unanchored on trouble seas.We may never be allowed ashore

As I look back I can see the hunger,the pain,the disgrace.The bewildering roads aimless nad reckless.
